About SKURI Taxi

SKURI Taxi is a campus taxi call app designed exclusively for Sungkyul University students. Our service provides a convenient and safe way to request taxi rides within and around campus.

With SKURI Taxi, students can quickly book rides, track their driver's location, and enjoy a seamless transportation experience tailored to the university community.

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

SKURI Taxi is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the necessary information to provide our service:

  • Email Address: We collect your Sungkyul University email address (@sungkyul.ac.kr) through Google login for authentication purposes only.
  • Location Data: We collect your real-time location information to facilitate taxi dispatch and provide accurate pickup services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Verifying your status as a Sungkyul University student
  • Connecting you with available taxi drivers
  • Providing accurate pickup and drop-off services
  • Improving our service quality

Data Sharing

We value your privacy. We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information with any third parties. Your data is used solely within our app to provide the taxi service.

Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can contact us at any time at support.skuri@gmail.com, and we will promptly remove all your information from our systems.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use SKURI Taxi?

SKURI Taxi is exclusively available to Sungkyul University students. You must have a valid @sungkyul.ac.kr email address to register and use the service.

How do I request a taxi?

Simply open the app, log in with your Sungkyul University Google account, allow location access, and tap the request button. A nearby driver will be dispatched to your location.

Is my location data safe?

Yes, absolutely. We only use your location data to provide the taxi service. Your location information is not shared with third parties and is handled with the highest security standards.
